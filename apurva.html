Product Process

How I approach new products

My default mode is: understand the real problem, keep the docs simple, and make sure design, engineering, and stakeholders are all solving the same thing.

1 · Discover
Listen before building

Talk to users, stakeholders, and data. Map workflows, find friction, and separate “what people say they want” from what they actually do.

2 · Define
Write the simplest possible PRD

Clarify the problem, constraints, assumptions, and guardrails. Capture jobs-to-be-done, user stories, metrics, and a phased scope that engineering can trust.

3 · Design
Prototype early, refine later

Start with low-fidelity flows to align on direction. Move into Figma with clear states, edge cases, and realistic data. Validate with quick reviews and feedback loops.

4 · Deliver
Ship in slices, not in chunks

Break work into clean increments, collaborate closely with engineering, and track usage against the original problem. Adjust roadmap based on what actually works.
